Welcome to futura

At Futura, our goal is to establish an environment where gender-segregated data is standard practice. Developed by Boston-based university students, we are driven by the potential to bring about enduring transformations in urban planning and design, focusing on elevating awareness about the importance of closing the gender-data gap. The absence of data considering women not only directs us towards a world where environments cater predominantly to men, but also suggests a societal shift where such a reality no longer belongs!
